About

Beyond the
keyword search.

I partner with CEOs and Revenue Leaders in SMBs to headhunt GTM talent who excel in key leadership traits that go deeper than what a keyword search can uncover. The conversation starts with character, and the traits that determine whether someone will perform in your environment. I'll challenge your thinking on who you need, and how you attract them.

My recruitment career began at Manpower Ireland in 2010, on a Scandinavian contract desk for IBM. That foundation took me to Melbourne, where I spent eight years moving from SAP implementation hiring to leading a data science and analytics team for a publicly listed global agency. I came home to Ireland with my family in 2018, and founded Trive in 2021.
